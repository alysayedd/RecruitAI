Data Scientist - Machine Learning

Company: Helio Analytics
Location: Dubai, UAE (Onsite)
Type: Full-time

About the Role
Join our analytics team to build ML models that drive product decisions across our fintech platform. You will work on credit scoring, fraud detection, and customer segmentation.

Responsibilities
- Design and train ML models (classification, regression, ranking)
- Run A/B tests and analyse experiment results
- Build data pipelines in Python and SQL
- Deploy models with MLflow and monitor drift
- Communicate findings to non-technical stakeholders

Required Skills
- 3+ years of experience in data science or ML engineering
- Strong Python (pandas, scikit-learn, NumPy)
- Solid SQL and experience with large datasets
- Statistical inference and experiment design
- Experience deploying at least one ML model to production

Nice to Have
- Deep learning frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow)
- LLM fine-tuning or RAG systems
- Cloud ML platforms (SageMaker, Vertex AI)
- Arabic NLP

Education
Master's or Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related quantitative field.
